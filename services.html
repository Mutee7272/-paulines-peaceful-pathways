Services

Care designed for adults (18+) — respectful, collaborative, and effective.

Psychiatric Evaluation & Diagnosis

Comprehensive assessments to understand your history, symptoms, goals, and to form a clear diagnosis and treatment plan.

  • Initial intake and clinical history
  • Collaborative goal setting
  • Personalized recommendations

Medication Management

Thoughtful prescribing and ongoing monitoring to maximize benefit and minimize side effects. Education and shared decisions are central.

  • Medication review and safety
  • Effectiveness tracking
  • Education & support

ADHD Treatment (Adults)

Evaluation and tailored treatment for adult ADHD to support focus, organization, and follow‑through.

  • Diagnostic clarification
  • Medication options and monitoring
  • Behavioral strategies

Anxiety Treatment

Evidence‑based approaches to manage worry, panic, and physical symptoms, with skills to restore balance.

  • Individualized care plans
  • Medication when appropriate
  • Stress management techniques

Depression Treatment

Support to reduce symptoms, improve functioning, and rebuild hope using evidence‑based care.

  • Comprehensive evaluation
  • Medication management
  • Wellness strategies
