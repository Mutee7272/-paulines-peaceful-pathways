FAQs

Do you see adults only?
Yes. The practice serves adults aged 18 and older.
Do you accept insurance?
Yes. Medicare, Medicaid, BCBS, UHC, Optum, Cigna, Aetna, Humana, Tricare, First Choice Health, Regence, and Premera. Please call to verify your benefits.
Do you offer telehealth?
Telehealth may be available depending on clinical needs and state regulations. Please call to discuss.
What should I bring to my first visit?
A list of current medications, past treatments, relevant records, and your insurance card.
