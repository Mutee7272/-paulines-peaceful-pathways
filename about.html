About Pauline

MSN, APRN, PMHNP — Patient‑centered psychiatric care for adults 18+

Compassionate, evidence‑based care

Pauline S Mead is a board‑certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP) with a Master of Science in Nursing (MSN). She provides compassionate, patient‑centered care with a focus on helping individuals find peace, balance, and improved mental health through professional treatment and guidance. Pauline believes in empowering patients on their mental health journey with dignity, respect, and evidence‑based care.

  • Board‑certified PMHNP
  • Adults (18+) — ADHD, Anxiety, Depression
  • Medication management and evaluations

Care philosophy

Respect & dignity

Every patient is treated with empathy, cultural humility, and respect.

Evidence‑based

Treatment decisions are grounded in current psychiatric best practices.

Collaboration

Shared decision‑making and clear education at each step of care.
